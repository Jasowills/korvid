# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Developer/power user running a personal AI assistant on their local machine. Context: sitting at their desk, terminal open, wants hands-free voice control and a visual command center to monitor and interact with their AI.

## Product Purpose

Korvid is a Jarvis-style personal AI assistant that runs locally. It provides voice control (wake word, clap activation), a visual dashboard for monitoring, and tool execution (screenshot, clipboard, files, commands). Success: the user feels like they have a living, responsive AI companion that's always ready.

## Brand Personality

Quiet confidence. Monochrome brutalism meets liquid glass. The Orb is the sole visual motif, glowing Sheen blue when active. No mascot, no warmth theater. Direct, capable, always-on.

## Anti-references

- Jarvis fan-art with blue holograms and Iron Man UI chrome
- Generic SaaS dashboards with cards, gradients, and cheerful onboarding
- AI assistants with cute names, emojis, or forced personality
- Dark mode that's just light mode with inverted colors
- Glassmorphism overuse (blurred everything, no contrast)

## Design Principles

1. **The Orb is the brand.** One glowing sphere, zero alternatives. Everything else is secondary.
2. **Monochrome first, Sheen accent earned.** White on black. The blue (#7C8CFF) appears only when the system is alive.
3. **Glass with purpose.** Backdrop blur on panels that float over the dark void. Not decorative glass.
4. **Quiet when idle, loud when active.** The dashboard breathes. Still darkness at rest, Sheen pulse on activity.
5. **Command center, not consumer app.** Dense information, no wasted space, no hand-holding.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA contrast for text (4.5:1 minimum)
- Reduced motion: disable particle animations, glow pulses
- Keyboard navigable panels
- Screen reader labels on status indicators
